Cybersecurity Club Summer Meeting


I hope everyone is having a good start to the summer semester. Just wanted to let everyone know that our next official club meeting will be Thursday 6/20/19 @ 05:30 pm. I will let you know which room as soon as one is arranged. Amanda Szampias who has agreed to lead our CCDC team in the upcoming semesters will be speaking. She will be going over questions on the competition, and tryout process. She will also be going over Stegnography and some other concepts.

Prior to that meeting we will be having a club voice call on the Discord server on Thursday 6/13/19 @ 07:30pm. Amanda and I will both be there to answer questions. I want to see how this media works for possible virtual meetings in the future. So please come with any questions you may have about the CCDC, ideas for future meetings, or anything interesting involving cybersecurity you have found recently you’d like to share. Call starts promptly at 07:30 pm. If you have not yet joined the Discord, there is an invite below.

Don’t forget about the competition going on in the Discord server. Remember, greater participation equals greater rewards. Furthermore, all questions may be answered at any time, not just the most recent!

I look forward to seeing you all soon.

- Mindy
Tri-C CyberSecurity Club President
