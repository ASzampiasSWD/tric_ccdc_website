Alternate - 4 Spots


Each CCDC Team is allowed up to four Alternates.

What You Get

  • Go to CCDC Practices
  • Private Discord Access
  • Name on a Paper
  • Low Probability of Playing in a CCDC Competition
  • Not your best choice

You have to volunteer to be an Alternate. This may be a good option for students who want access to training materials but didn't make the team.

If one of the 8-Person team members is sick or can't make it for a competition, you will fill in. In this event you will be X Admin and your Alternate title can be thrown away.
