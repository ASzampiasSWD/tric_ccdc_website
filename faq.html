FAQ


How does the Tryout Process work?

Tryouts will be planned in November - December. If more than three students want a certain position, they will battle it out in a mock-competition. The two exceptions are Team Captain and Firewall Admin. If more than one person wants the Team Captain position, we will have them turn in resumes, participate in a mock-interview, and turn in a written work. (Paper, Blog Post, article to display their written skills). Firewall Admin contenders must display higher skill level and accomplishment than Amanda before October 1.

What are Team Rules?

No Ultimatums or Sabotaging. Examples include going AWOL during competition and ignoring the team captain and teammates. You will be kicked off.

What is Invitationals?

This is a Practice CCDC event that may or may not happen around November. We will pick participants and do a switch-around strategy where everyone gets time.
