How do I make the team?

We are looking for three qualities:

1. Ability to Self-Learn

"Knowing is NOT the most important thing. To be able to FIND OUT is more important than knowing." - Sugata Mitra

Being able to take a problem and find a solution is an important skill in IT. Can you independently research how to install SSH on Linux? Can you use Google, Youtube, and other resources? Can you spend the time required to figure something out? We are looking for individuals who can self-teach themselves. During the competition you may need to figure out how to do something new within an hour.

2. Full-Time Student Status

You must take 12 credits during Spring Semester 2020. You can be part-time for Qualifiers but must be full-time for Midwest Regionals. If your concerned about full-time status talk to Amanda. Still tryout for the team anyway.

3. Walk the Walk - Hands on Skills

Prove you can do the job. Write down documentation. Set up a lab. Do injects. The best person to manage a web server is someone who manages a web server. The best person to secure PHP code is someone who codes in PHP. etc.

How to Improve Your Chances

  • CCDC Meeting attendance and Discord presence - We need to know who you are. We don't want to give a spot to a ghost who will disappear.
  • Build your own Lab in VMWare Workstation, VMWare Player or VirtualBox. Install Operating Systems used in previous CCDC Competitions.
  • Practice and document Injects from previous years.
  • Learn how to use and configure services. Examples include SSH, FTP, HTTP, DNS, Email.
  • Help teammates who have questions.
